Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan 1, 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect information you directly provide to us, including your name, email, and any match logs or performance metrics you input into Sportlytics.

2. Data Usage

Your performance data is used to calculate analytics, award badges, and display leaderboards. If your profile is set to public, other members can view your efficiency score and stats.

3. Security

We secure your data using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt) and JWT tokens. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure.

4. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated match data from the Settings panel in your Dashboard.
